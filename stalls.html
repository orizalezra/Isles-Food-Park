Maestro Food Hub

Maestro Food Hub features savory and delicious but affordable homemade dishes like Bicol Express. But their best sellers are sizzling dishes in sizzling plate like Sizzlng Sisig, Sizzling Pork Steak and many more. That's why they are the "Home of the Best Sizzling Meal."

The Chef Fusion

Giving you the taste of the authentic Japanese dishes and the experience of eating in a authentic Japanese restaurant but at affordable price.

Bam-Bam's Kitchen

Their Sizzling Sisig is another level, making it their best seller. And for the dessert, their Banana Con Yelo is also on another level because of the freshness of the fruits that are used. This dessert will make you refreshed like you just took a shower.

Cyclene's Takamyaki

Macha Brew

Our favorite Japanese streetfood is now available in Isles Food Park but in affordable price plus their healthy drink. This is a satisfying combo.

Buko Twizt

Cool and refreshing buko juice with a twist is a perfect match to beat the heat. Every Filipino loves buko juice. And who doesn't love to have it with a twist?

Bro's Inasal

You will be fully satisfied by Bro's Inasal's affordable unli rice meals. If you are craving for "Mang Inasal" but don't have enough budget, then this one is for you. This stall is recommended for you if you love to eat a lot but you also love to spend less.

Waff 'N Chip

Who doesn't like waffle and coffee for breakfast? of course we all love waffle and coffee for breakfast. Perfect to start the day before getting into our class.

NY Kitchen

M&M Foodtrip

Their products are the best if you are looking for a snack while studying. Their fries+burger+shake combo meals are just delightfully tasty.

Happy Tummy

Craving something tasty while hitting the books? Their budget-friendly meals and fried noodles are just what you need.
